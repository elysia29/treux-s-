Welcome to Reality Dewdrops

Bringing awareness to pressing social and community issues through impactful stories.

INTRODUCTION :

A critical health issue is affecting a community called Sitio Bebe. Due to the absence of a natural water stream,
the local administration must rely on drilling wells or importing clean water for its residents.
This scarcity often leads to severe drought, particularly during the summer months, creating a dire need for sustainable solutions.

OUR CHOSEN CATEGORY :

TED Talks: We focus on delivering engaging short talks and presentations to inform and educate audiences. Our mission is to spread awareness about critical issues, encouraging thoughtful dialogue and action.

TARGET AUDIENCE

**Demographics**: Students to adults (6-70 years old). **Interests**:

Social and community issues / Learning new concepts / Academic knowledge and growth

AUDIENCE NEEDS

Our videos aim to serve academic purposes, acting as a guide for projects and other educational activities.
- TED Talks-style content captures curiosity and fosters a deeper understanding of Sitio Bebe’s challenges.
- Viewers will gain valuable knowledge about the community’s situation, promoting awareness and advocacy.
- We aim to inspire audiences to think critically and take meaningful actions toward solutions.

About Us: Reality Dewdrops

Welcome to Reality Dewdrops! We’re Group 4. Reality dewdrops represent small moments of insight or truths that come out of life’s complexities. These “dewdrops” of knowledge provide insight into world realities while keeping freshness and purity. We’re here to inspire and make a positive impact.

But we’re not just about talks; we care about the environment too! That’s why our product is reusable tote bags that help you reduce plastic waste. Our bags are a fun and eco-friendly way to make a difference every day.

At Reality Dewdrops, we believe in mixing great ideas with actions that matter. Whether you’re here to learn something new from TED Talks or pick up a tote to help save the planet, we’re here for you. Since we really care about the current condition of our planet, every effort and drop of knowledge counts.

Our Product

We offer eco-friendly, reusable tote bags designed to reduce waste and promote sustainable living. These bags are perfect for daily use and help minimize single-use plastic.
